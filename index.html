Qazaq national jewelry

Handmade Kazakh jewelry out of fashion and timeless. Unusually beautiful jewelry made of precious metals, made in the classic Kazakh style, light, almost airy, literally filled with national flavor.

About us

What makes us special?

Jewelry is one of the most common and honorable gifts for women. This is directly related not only to the love of female representatives for jewelry, but also to the ancient customs of the people. Ornaments and decoration materials embody not only a decorative, but also a protective function.Of all the precious metals, Kazakhs prefer to make jewelry out of silver. Kazakh silver jewelry amazes with its majesty and beauty, due to which they are in high demand. Our country and people are rich in wonderful traditions, and we wanted to preserve them, since there is no future without the past.
